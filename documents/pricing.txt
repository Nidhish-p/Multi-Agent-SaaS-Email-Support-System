**Overview**

[Platform] offers three plans designed to scale with your agency. All plans include access to the core platform. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your account dashboard.

---

**Plans**

**Free Plan — $0/month**
Best for individuals exploring the platform or just getting started.
- Foundational model selection (OpenAI models only)
- Template prompting
- Limited versioning (up to 3 versions per agent)
- Train with sample data (no custom uploads)
- 1 active agent
- Community support only

**Pro Plan — $49/month**
Best for growing agencies managing multiple clients.
- Everything in the Free plan
- All foundational models including open-source and third-party
- Advanced tooling integration (custom tools + Bland.ai voice)
- Prompt improvement system
- Enhanced versioning (unlimited versions)
- Train with custom data (up to 5 knowledge bases)
- Up to 10 active agents
- Email support with 48-hour response time

**Premium Plan — $99/month**
Best for established agencies with high-volume needs.
- Everything in the Pro plan
- Priority support with 12-hour response time
- Exclusive and early-access templates
- Custom tooling integration with dedicated setup assistance
- Unlimited knowledge bases
- Unlimited active agents
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom onboarding session

---

**Billing FAQs**

**How am I billed?**
All plans are billed monthly. Annual billing with a 20% discount is available on Pro and Premium plans.

**Can I upgrade mid-cycle?**
Yes. When you upgrade, you are charged the prorated difference immediately and your new features are activated instantly.

**Can I add more seats?**
Team seats are available on Pro and Premium plans. Each additional seat is $15/month on Pro and $20/month on Premium.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
[Platform] accepts all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) as well as PayPal.

**Is there a free trial for paid plans?**
Yes. Pro and Premium plans include a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

**What happens when I hit my agent limit on the Free plan?**
You will be prompted to upgrade to Pro. Existing agents remain active but you cannot create new ones until you upgrade.

**Are there refunds?**
[Platform] offers a 14-day money-back guarantee on your first paid subscription. Contact support@[platform.com] to request a refund.

---

**How to Upgrade**
1. Log in to your [Platform] dashboard
2. Navigate to Settings → Billing
3. Select your desired plan and confirm
4. Your new features are available immediately
