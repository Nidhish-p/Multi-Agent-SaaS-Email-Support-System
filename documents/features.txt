**[Platform]: Product Features & Functionality**

---

**Overview**

[Platform] is a comprehensive AI agent creation and deployment platform built specifically for AI Automation Agency owners. It provides everything needed to build, manage, and deliver AI solutions to clients — from model selection to deployment — all in one place.

---

**Core Features**

**Foundational Model Selection**
[Platform] supports a wide range of foundational models, giving you complete flexibility to choose the best fit for your use case:
- OpenAI models: GPT-4, GPT-4 Turbo, GPT-3.5 Turbo
- Open-source models: Llama 3, Mistral
- Third-party models: Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini Pro
You can switch models at any time without rebuilding your agent from scratch.

**Tooling & Actions**
[Platform] allows your agents to perform real-world actions through integrations:
- Voice calling via Bland.ai — agents can make and receive calls
- Custom tool integration — bring your own tools via API
- Platform connectors — interact with CRMs, helpdesks, and other third-party systems
- Web search and data retrieval capabilities

**Prompt Engineering**
- Templated prompts using the latest industry best practices
- Prompt improvement system that suggests optimizations based on your agent's goals
- Version-controlled prompt history so you can roll back anytime
- Support for system prompts, few-shot examples, and dynamic variables

**Zero Downtime Versioning**
[Platform] is the only platform that lets you update your agents offline and push changes live seamlessly:
- Test changes in a staging environment before going live
- Instantly roll back to a previous version if needed
- No service interruption for your clients during updates

**RAG — Train with Your Own Data**
Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) allows your agents to answer questions grounded in your own documents:
- Upload PDFs, text files, CSVs, and web content
- Automatic chunking, embedding, and indexing
- Agents retrieve relevant context before generating responses
- Supports multiple knowledge bases per agent

**Templates**
- Pre-built agent templates for industries including customer support, sales, legal, and HR
- Fully customizable starting points to reduce development time
- New templates added monthly by the [Platform] team
